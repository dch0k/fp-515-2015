About

In this modern technology world every retail company tracks their performance and service based on the customer satisfaction. The best way is by conducting a survey to know about customer buying behavior and following market trends. Eventually we need data to perform analytics on product sales. To gather data a web application designed to register a customer, and then uploading their monthly bank statements of credit card and debit card purchases. This web application will only generate a data collection model and in turn rewards points are awarded for every upload of statement file. I have labeled this project as “Mondo Data”, here Mondo (adj.) means big
